Various Jack Fruit Variety.

Jack Fruit-Black Gold

The Black Gold Jackfruit is a vigorous Jackfruit originating from Qld. The Black gold has a dense, manageable canopy that can be pruned to keep it compact (2m x 2m) so perfect for those not wanting a tree to take up too much space. The Fruit of the black gold average about 6kg and have deep orange colour.

Jack Fruit-Golden Nugget

The Golden Nugget is a fast growing tree in Jackfruit family, albeit it is considered a dwarfing variety and can be kept in a container for a very long time. The tree has a distinctive dark green, rounded leaf. It forms a dense, spreading canopy, which is easily maintained at a height and spread of 2 to 2.5m.
